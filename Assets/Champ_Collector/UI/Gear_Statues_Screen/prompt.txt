In a vibrant and adult fantasy world, a concept art style illustration of three large white stone slate statues on top of wide pedestals. Each depicts a humanoid creature donning one colored item: one holds a red intricate shield to their front, another a red heavy sledgehammer they lean on, and the last wears red boots. They surround a large bonfire, highlighting their details. The statues huge frames almost loom over, intimidating anyone approaching. A blurry and shadowy thin forest at the back of the statues.
Saturated colors, as the surrounding environment fades into a dreamy haze. The bonfire seem to make the surroundings darker, accentuating the shape of the statues.
